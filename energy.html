Project information

  • Category: Web design
  • Language: HTML, CSS, Django (Python)
  • Project date: Aug 2023 - Jun 2024
  • Project URL: COMING SOON!

Details

This is a web app developed for the ISF Academy. It is designed to track students' daily energy usage and provide them with a visual representation of their energy consumption. The app also includes a student leaderboard to encourage students to save energy. The project is currently in development and is expected to be completed by June 2024.

Made with Bootstrap.
