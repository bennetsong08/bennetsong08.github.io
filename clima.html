Details

Clima is a mobile app that allows users to track their carbon footprint. It lets users set carbon targets, add entries, view their progress and statistics, and more. The app is built using Flutter and Firebase. It is currently available on the App Store and as a direct APK download for Android.


The idea for this project first came to me back in 2022, when I was still learning the basics of computer programming. I wanted to create a project that could combine my passion for coding with my interest in environmental sustainability. After building my knowledge of computer programming and gaining the necessary skills, I was finally able to create a mobile app that could help users track their carbon footprint and make more sustainable choices in their daily lives. I started working on the app in June 2024 and have been continuously updating it since then.
